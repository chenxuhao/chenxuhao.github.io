About

I am an assistant research scientist at College of Computer, National University of Defense Technology (NUDT). I work on computer architecture and computer systems, with an emphasis on massively parallel accelerators. My recent work focuses on efficient processing of emerging irregular algorithms (big-data analytics and machine learning). I received B.S. and Ph.D. degree from NUDT in 2009 and 2014 respectively. I spent two years (from 2012 to 2014) as a visiting Ph.D. student at the IMPACT Research group in University of Illinois Urbana-Champaign.
